## Exercise
## Confounding variables

## Instructions
## - Identify which option could be a confounding variable
##   in this experiment.
## - Select one answer.

## Scenario
## You are investigating the relationship between:
## - Neighborhood residence
## - Lung capacity

## Study design:
## - 30 people from Neighborhood A (located near a highway)
## - 30 people from Neighborhood B (not near a highway)
## - Both groups have similar smoking habits
## - Both groups have a similar gender breakdown

## Your task:
## Choose which variable could act as a confounding variable.

## Possible Answers
## - Lung capacity
## - Neighborhood
## - Air pollution
## - Smoking status
## - Gender

## MY SOLUTION:

## - Air pollution
