## Exercise
## Significance levels vs. p-values

## Instructions
## - Match the statements to the correct bucket:
##   Significance level
##   or
##   p-value

## MY SOLUTION:

## Significance level
## - Also known as alpha.
## - This is a threshold assigned to represent the probability
##   of falsely rejecting the null hypothesis.
## - This is set before data collection.

## p-value
## - To reject the null hypothesis this value must be less than
##   or equal to alpha.
## - This metric is calculated after data has been collected.
## - This describes the probability of obtaining results at least
##   as extreme as those observed.
