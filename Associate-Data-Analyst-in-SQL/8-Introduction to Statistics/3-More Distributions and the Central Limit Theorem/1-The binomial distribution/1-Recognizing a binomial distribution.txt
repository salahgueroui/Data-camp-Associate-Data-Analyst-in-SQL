## Exercise
## Recognizing a binomial distribution

## Instructions
## - Recall that a binomial distribution counts
##   the number of successes in independent trials.
## - Choose the scenario that best describes
##   a binomial distribution.
## - Select one correct answer.

## Given Information
## - A binomial distribution involves:
##   • A fixed number of trials
##   • Independent events
##   • Two possible outcomes (success/failure)
##   • Counting the number of successes

## Question
## Which scenario represents a binomial distribution?

## Possible Answers
## - The probability of a train arriving in under 10 minutes.
## - The probability of a man's height being 175 centimeters or less.
## - The probability of matching four numbers in the lottery.
## - The probability of the temperature being more than 80 degrees Celsius.

## MY SOLUTION:

## - The probability of matching four numbers in the lottery.
