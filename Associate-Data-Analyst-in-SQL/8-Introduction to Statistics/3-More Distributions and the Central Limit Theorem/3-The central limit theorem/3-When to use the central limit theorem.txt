## Exercise
## When to use the central limit theorem

## Instructions
## - Select the scenario that would require the use of
##   the central limit theorem (CLT)
##   to produce summary statistics that are normally distributed.
## - Choose one answer.

## Given Information
## - The CLT is applied when we want the sampling distribution
##   of a summary statistic (such as the mean or proportion)
##   to be approximately normal.
## - The decision depends on:
##   • Whether we are working with a sample or a full population  
##   • The type of statistic being calculated  
##   • The sample size  

## Possible Answers
## - Finding the mean IQ of all 100 students in a high school.
## - Measuring the standard deviation of household income
##   in a road with 20 houses.
## - Determining the percentage of adults in the USA
##   that have been diagnosed with Type 2 Diabetes.
## - Counting the number of babies born with blue eyes
##   in a hospital within one day.

## MY SOLUTION:

##   that have been diagnosed with Type 2 Diabetes.
