## Exercise
## Probability with a uniform distribution

## Instructions
## - Use the continuous uniform distribution shown.
## - Calculate the probability that the next train arrives
##   between 5 and 13 minutes.
## - Select the correct answer from the options below.

## Given Information
## - The wait time follows a continuous uniform distribution.
## - The minimum wait time is 0 minutes.
## - The maximum wait time is 20 minutes.
## - The graph shows a constant probability density
##   across the interval from 0 to 20 minutes.

## Question
## What is the probability that the next train
## arrives between 5 and 13 minutes?

## Possible Answers
## - 5%
## - 40%
## - 80%
## - 30%

## MY SOLUTION:

(13-5)*0.05
## - 40%
